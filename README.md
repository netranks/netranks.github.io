## What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the discipline of optimizing your brand's visibility in AI-powered search engines. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on ranking in blue links, GEO ensures your brand is cited, mentioned, and accurately represented when users ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, or SearchGPT questions in your industry.
